What is a Model UN?

Model United Nations, often referred to as MUN, is a simulation of the United Nations and it strives at educating students about current global affairs. Through a combination of debate and negotiation, students are exposed to an environment that closely deals with international affairs and relations and work to solve pressing issues.

Apart from this opportunity serving as a learning experience, delegates constantly improve their interpersonal and public speaking skills.
