OakMUN is a simulation of the largest international organization, the United Nations, which aims to educate students about current events, topics in international relations, diplomacy, and pressing world issues. Student delegates take on roles of international diplomats and ambassadors in various organizations and agencies of the United Nations. OakMUN challenges students to broaden their outlook, undertake responsibility, and holistically develop their skills. It motivate students to learn, to share experiences with like minded students, and ultimately, to grow into successful leaders of the future.
